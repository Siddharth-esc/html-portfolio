The Best Movies According to siddharth

My top 3 movies of all time

Night crawler

"Nightcrawler" casts Jake Gyllenhaal in a mesmerizing role as Lou Bloom, a freelance videographer who descends into the dark world of crime journalism in Los Angeles. With its tense atmosphere and morally ambiguous protagonist, the film offers a disturbing glimpse into the lengths one will go for success in the cutthroat media industry.

stay

"Stay" stars Ryan Gosling as a troubled psychology student grappling with reality and perception after a mysterious car accident. The film's mind-bending narrative blurs the lines between dreams and reality, keeping audiences guessing until the very end. Gosling's intense portrayal adds depth to the psychological thriller, making it a haunting exploration of identity and existential angst.

Demolition

"Demolition" features Jake Gyllenhaal as Davis Mitchell, a man who begins to unravel emotionally after losing his wife in a tragic car accident. As he navigates grief in unconventional ways, such as disassembling and destroying objects, the film delves into themes of catharsis and self-discovery. Gyllenhaal's poignant performance captures the complexities of mourning and rebuilding life from the ground up, making "Demolition" a compelling exploration of loss, healing, and unexpected connections.
